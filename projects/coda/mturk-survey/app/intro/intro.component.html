Before we can get started, we'll show you how the task works and point out a few notable details. In this task, you'll be presented with a series of {{ numObjects }} objects, and asked to rate the frequency that the object is that color. You are allowed to skip any objects that you're not familiar with, at which point you'll be presented with a new object. Note that skipped objects are not counted toward your HIT completion.

For each of the listed colors, you'll use sliders to indicate how frequently the object is that color. Use a scale of 0-5 in your ratings where 0 corresponds to sometimes and 5 corresponds to almost always. Note that extremely rare or extraordinary events correspond to 0 on this scale. All responses will be normalized as a distribution over the selected colors. For example, a rating of 5/5 for 2 colors is identical to a rating of 1/5 for both colors (both will be 50/50).
